Last Updated: January 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Chamith Madushan Athukorala ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile applications.

We do not collect personal data like names or phone numbers directly. However, our apps use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Our apps use Google AdMob to show advertisements. To provide relevant ads, Google may collect and use data such as:

  • Your device's Advertising ID (AAID).

You can learn more about how Google uses your data by visiting: Google’s Advertising Privacy & Terms

3. Analytics and Performance

We use basic analytics to understand how our apps are performing. This data is anonymous and helps us fix bugs and improve the user experience.

4. Third-Party Links

Our apps may contain links to the Google Play Store. These external sites are not operated by us; we strongly advise you to review their Privacy Policies.

5. Children's Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You can reset your advertising ID or opt-out of personalized ads via your device settings: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization.
